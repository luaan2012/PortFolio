NerdStore distributed systems/microservices

This ecommerce platform leverages a variety of cutting-edge technologies to provide a seamless, high-speed, and reliable shopping experience. Our tech stack includes EasyNetQ, FluentValidation, Polly, NetDevPack, MoreLinq, gRPC, Entity Framework (EF), MediatR, Dapper, JSON Web Tokens (JWT), Docker, NGINX, and Refit.

The project initially started with a template but underwent extensive modifications to cater to our specific needs. It is built as a monolithic application using .NET 6.

The inspiration for this project came from a highly recommended course titled ".NET Core Enterprise Applications." It's a testament to the latest advancements in web technology, enabling us to offer fluid navigation, lightning-fast performance, and a secure shopping environment.

Our ecommerce solution seamlessly integrates various APIs and the aforementioned technologies to provide:

  • Fluid and intuitive user experience.
  • Lightning-fast page load times.
  • Robust security with JWT authentication.
  • Efficient backend operations using Dapper and Entity Framework.
  • Containerized deployment with Docker for scalability.
  • Optimized server performance through NGINX.
  • API communication made easy with Refit.

Our commitment to utilizing these technologies ensures a modern, efficient, and secure online shopping experience for our customers.

MyFinances v1

MyFinances v1 is a comprehensive financial management dashboard built in .NET 6 for those who aim to take control of their daily expenses and earnings. This tool empowers you to stay informed about your daily financial activities and effectively manage your finances.

MyFinances v1 is designed as a monolithic application.

Whether it's keeping tabs on expenses, monitoring income, or setting and achieving financial goals, this application has you covered.

Key Features:

  • Expense Tracking: Easily record and categorize your daily expenditures to gain insights into your spending habits.
  • Income Management: Keep track of your daily earnings and ensure a comprehensive overview of your financial inflow.
  • Goal Setting: Set financial objectives and monitor your progress toward achieving them, all within the same platform.
  • Debt Management: Stay organized by tracking your outstanding debts and establishing a clear plan to manage them.
  • Finances v1 is the ideal companion for anyone who desires a simple, intuitive, and powerful tool to maintain control over their financial life. Start your journey towards financial stability and smart money management today with MyFinances v1!
